HUSET KBH - FILM EVENTS - HUSET BIOGRAF

Huset Biograf is located in Huset KBH in the centre of Copenhagen. We have many film events daily and create memorable experiences for fans of cult, grindhouse, 80's cult films, underground, experimental and midnight movie-style films. We welcome tourists and expats to join our Film Events which are also held through English.
